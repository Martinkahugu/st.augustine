Self Help Group

ST. AUGUSTINE KIMBO CATHOLIC CHURCH S.H.G

St. Augustine Catholic Church Kimbo Self-help group is a model finacial service provider owned by Christian communities and managed on Christian values of love and charity Since its inception in June 2006, it has continued to offer viable financial services to members within our area & beyond for sustainable economic & social growth. As a demonstration of our motto “Love and Empowering Lives”the group is opens not only to the Catholics but also Non-Catholics, who are willing to be governed by catholic teachings and ethics.

VISION:

To be the leading financial service provider to the Christian community in the Archdiocese of Nairobi and its environs.

MISSION:

To offer outstanding services thus enabling the neighbourhood community grow socially and economically and give returns to members.

OBJECTIVES
  1. Mobilization of savings thus creating a source of funds from which loans can be made
  2. Issuance of loans at an affordable rate of interest therby enabling members to develop themselves
  3. To offer continous education programmes on savings and proper use of credit aimed at promoting economic growth of members.
Our core values
  1. Integrity
  2. Honest
  3. Christian solidarity
  4. Quality service
  5. Accountability
  6. Focused and reliability

Products and Services

  1. Normal loans
  2. Emergency loan -Payable within 1yr equal or greater than 100,000
  3. Development loan
  4. Agri-business payable maximum 2yrs
  5. Education loan payable within 1 yr for primary and secondary, 2yrs for post secondary education.
  6. Loans are granted at a low interest of 1% per month on reducing balance and should be fully guaranteed.

MEMBERSHIP

  1. A person of either gender shall be eligible for membership.
  2. Open to both Catholics and Non-Catholic.
  3. One must have attained 18 years, of good character and of sound mind.
  4. Fill registration application form.
  5. A copy of national identity card.
  6. 2 coloured passports.
  7. Each applicant will pay one off refundable registration fee of ksh.300 and a monthly minimum contribution of Ksh 200 (10 shares @ Ksh 20).
  8. An applicant shall be admitted to membership on being accepted by majority vote of management committee.
