Our History

CMA is as old as st Augustine church. It was started under the chairmanship of one Mr Gabriel Mureithi in 1999. It's current chairman is Mr. John Hinga who happens to be also the parish chairman who is serving his second term in that capacity. CMA has a membership of 50 registered members and the active is about 35. It has a representation of almost all jumuias. It has a strong Motto: GOOD FAMILY, GOOD CHURCH, GOOD SOCIETY. Members usually provide internal security duration major church functions e.g harambee and patron's day. CMA cerebrates to major days in an year ie month of March st Joseph husband of mother Mary and 1st May st Joseph the worker. Men are the major pillar of the family n the church.
