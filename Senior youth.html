Senior youth


"I urge you to let yourselves be shaped by [the Holy Spirit] in order to be messengers of divine love, capable of building a future of hope for all humanity. Pope's address to the youth of the world"

Our History

St. Augustine Youth Group is a vibrant intercultural spiritual group enjoying more than a decade of significance and relevance in the church, and is still going strong. Members fall between 18-35 years of age. We are an all-rounded group that revolves around Spiritual and Socio-economic activities that contribute to the overall well-being of our members

Our Goals

  1. We Aim at challenging the ourselves to lead a life of prayer; commitment to our faith and change of attitude.
  2. To be empowered to live as disciples of Jesus Christ in our world today.
  3. To draw more of us to responsible participation in the life, mission, and work of the Catholic faith community.
  4. To foster the total personal and spiritual growth of each young person.

"The Church is concerned for the whole person, addressing the young people's spiritual needs in the context of his/her whole life. The youth ministry fosters positive development and growth in both Christian discipleship and Catholic identity. Promoting the growth of young means addressing their unique developmental, social, and religious needs and nurturing the qualities or assets necessary for positive development. It also means addressing the objective obstacles to healthy growth that affect the lives of so many young people, such as poverty, racial discrimination, and social injustice, as well as the subjective obstacles to healthy growth such as the loss of a sense of sin, the influence of values promoted by the secular media, and the negative impact of the consumer mentality."

Our Activities

Under the Steward leadership of its 7 member council, who ensure that the real purpose of the group is achieved inline with the Laid down framework guided by:-

  1. The Catholic Church Doctrine
  2. The Saint Augustine Youth Constitution
  3. The Youth Action Plan(Calendar)

Spiritual

  1. Reading of the Word of God is an activity in our meetings. This helps the Word of God to be incarnated in the hearts of the youth.
  2. We recite the Rosary together every Wednesday and Bible study sharing at 1900hrs,spiritual talks guided by our Spiritual coordinator
  3. We sing the 1st mass that Starts at 0730hrs every Sunday and have a choir that is well- versed in Swahili, English and Cultural songs. We thank the Lord for the grace and wisdom in guiding us to making the mass lively.
  4. Attending Retreats

Economically

We are able to support ourselves through two major activities exclusively run by the youth themselves
  1. Production and subsequent sale of bulletins.
  2. Provision of refreshments in the form of porridge, Smokie and Eggs.

Social

Our group is sensitive to our social needs, and the needs of the Community at large, and once every quarter we engage in CSR
  1. Visiting Children Homes,visiting the sick and the old.
  2. Cleaning the Church
  3. Engages in sports every often, biannual birthday parties
