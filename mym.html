Saint Maria Goretti

The Patron Saint Of St. Augustine Catholic Church MYM

The Pope invites the youth to:
  1. Be salt of the earth and the light of the world
  2. Be builders and protagonists of the future
  3. Fight against drugs, alcoholism, sex and other regrettable vices
  4. Set for themselves the achievement of noble and high human and Christian ideals
  5. Undertake the path of conversion in order to attain a new heart and newness of life.
  6. Live in union with God, for only he can quench the thirst within their hearts
  7. Be committed Christians and collaborate within the parish community
  8. Be apostles for other young persons
  9. Be builders of a more just and brotherly society
  10. Love Jesus and Mother Mary and the Church

Meaning of MYM

MYM is a missionary animation to help young people to grow in their faith and fulfill their personal vocation in a universal spirit. It brings together those young persons who more deeply commit themselves to the missionary animation of the other youth. MYM is called youth because it is made up of adolescents and young persons.It helps the young person to live and exercise his/her leadership among the young.
It is called missionary because its members aim at being missionary (reaching out to others). MYM (leaven group) is made up of missionary youth, animated by the Holy Spirit, chosen by God, called by Jesus in order to work in his vineyard and sent to it, in order to witness the coherence between life and faith, always within a dimension of missionary service and animation.

Eligibility for membership

  1. All young people who wish to follow and serve Jesus Christ as missionaries to their communities and universal church.
  2. Children who are not anymore members of PMC because they are adolescent
The young people commit themselves to;
  1. Give testimony of true Christian life, through frequent reception of the sacrament
  2. Take part in weekly prayers for the youths and missions of the whole world
  3. Be missionary in the family and among friends and classmates

Our Models

  1. Mary Queen of the Apostles -22nd August
  2. St. Francis Xavier – 3rd December
  3. St. Teresa of the child Jesus – 1st October
The sky blue colour for MYM The choice of the sky blue colour for the uniform is inspired by the Blessed Virgin Mary who is the chief model of purity to MYM. Sky blue is the colour of Mary (Queen of Heaven). They should be inspired by the Purity of the Mother of God.

Our Activities

  1. One of the activities is doctrinal study on particular themes (missionary catechesis). This will help in deepening one’s awareness of Jesus and the Church. The aim is to help the young people render their mission with clear and motivated minds and be capable to give reasons for their faith.
  2. Reading of the Word of God is also an activity in their meetings. This will help the Word of God to be incarnated in the hearts of the youth.
  3. Other activities includes: prayer groups, retreats and recollections, adoration (encounter with the friend Jesus), family and missionary feasts, games, cultural day, sports day, helping others, visits to the needy, disabled, sick, prisoners, other youth groups, preparing animation materials, sales on behalf of the poor, foster and share experiences through writings etc.
